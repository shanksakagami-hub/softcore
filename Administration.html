About Our Administration

The administration of Softcore University is committed to providing effective leadership and management to support the university's mission of excellence in education, research, and community service. Our administrative structure is designed to facilitate efficient operations and promote a supportive environment for students, faculty, and staff.

Office of the Vice Chancellor

Prof. Jane Mwangi

Vice Chancellor

Prof. Jane Mwangi is a distinguished academic with over 25 years of experience in higher education leadership. She holds a Ph.D. in Educational Administration from Harvard University and has published extensively on higher education management and policy.

Email: vicechancellor@softcore.co.ke

Office: Administration Block, 3rd Floor

The Office of the Vice Chancellor provides strategic leadership to the university, overseeing all academic and administrative functions. The Vice Chancellor works closely with the University Council and Senate to develop and implement policies that advance the university's mission and vision.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Strategic planning and institutional development
  • Resource allocation and management
  • Academic leadership and quality assurance
  • External relations and partnerships
  • Institutional representation and advocacy

Finance Department

Mr. David Mutiso

Chief Finance Officer

Mr. David Mutiso is a seasoned financial administrator with extensive experience in higher education finance management. He holds an MBA in Finance and is a Certified Public Accountant with over 15 years of experience in financial management.

Email: finance@softcore.co.ke

Office: Administration Block, 2nd Floor

The Finance Department is responsible for the financial management of the university, ensuring fiscal responsibility, transparency, and accountability. The department oversees budgeting, accounting, financial reporting, and procurement processes.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Budget preparation and management
  • Financial accounting and reporting
  • Revenue collection and fee management
  • Procurement and asset management
  • Financial compliance and audit coordination

Human Resource Department

Ms. Sarah Ochieng

Director of Human Resources

Ms. Sarah Ochieng is an experienced HR professional with a strong background in higher education human resource management. She holds a Master's degree in Human Resource Management and has over 12 years of experience in the field.

Email: hr@softcore.co.ke

Office: Administration Block, 1st Floor

The Human Resource Department is responsible for all aspects of personnel management, including recruitment, professional development, performance management, and employee relations. The department works to create a supportive and inclusive work environment for all university employees.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Recruitment and selection of staff
  • Staff development and training
  • Performance management
  • Employee relations and welfare
  • Payroll administration

Research and Extension

Prof. Michael Kamau

Director of Research and Extension

Prof. Michael Kamau is a distinguished researcher with an impressive record of scholarly publications and grant acquisition. He holds a Ph.D. in Applied Sciences and has led numerous research initiatives with national and international impact.

Email: research@softcore.co.ke

Office: Research Complex, Ground Floor

The Research and Extension department promotes and supports research activities across the university, facilitating knowledge creation, innovation, and community engagement. The department manages research grants, coordinates collaborative research projects, and oversees the university's contribution to community development.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Research policy development and implementation
  • Research grants management
  • Intellectual property management
  • Research ethics oversight
  • Community outreach and extension services
