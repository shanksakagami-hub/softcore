About Softcore University

Softcore University is a premier institution committed to academic excellence, innovation, and research. Located in Nairobi, our university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across multiple disciplines. We strive to produce graduates who are well-prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving global society.
