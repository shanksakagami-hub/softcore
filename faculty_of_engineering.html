Welcome to the Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering at Softcore University is dedicated to producing skilled engineers equipped with theoretical knowledge and practical skills to address contemporary challenges in engineering. Our programs focus on innovation, sustainability, and industry-relevant skills, preparing graduates for successful careers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Prof. Robert Njoroge, Ph.D.

Dean, Faculty of Engineering

Prof. Njoroge brings over 20 years of experience in engineering education and research. He has led numerous innovative projects and published extensively in prestigious international journals. His vision is to establish the Faculty of Engineering as a center of excellence in research and innovation in East Africa.

Our Departments

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering focuses on the design, manufacturing, and operation of mechanical systems. Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience.

Programs Offered:

Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering

A four-year program that provides comprehensive education in mechanical engineering principles, design methodologies, and manufacturing techniques.

Duration: 4 Years
Delivery Mode: Full-time
Entry Requirements: C+ in KCSE
Key Subjects: Mathematics, Physics

Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering

An advanced program focusing on specialized areas such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and mechanical design.

Duration: 2 Years
Delivery Mode: Full-time/Part-time
Entry Requirements: Bachelor's degree in relevant field
Key Focus: Research and specialization

Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering prepares students for designing, constructing, and maintaining physical infrastructure. Our curriculum emphasizes sustainable development and innovative solutions for urban challenges.

Programs Offered:

Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering

A comprehensive program covering structural engineering, transportation systems, geotechnical engineering, water resources, and environmental engineering.

Duration: 4 Years
Delivery Mode: Full-time
Entry Requirements: C+ in KCSE
Key Subjects: Mathematics, Physics

Master of Engineering in Structural Engineering

An advanced program specializing in the analysis and design of structures, earthquake engineering, and advanced construction materials.

Duration: 2 Years
Delivery Mode: Full-time/Part-time
Entry Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
Key Focus: Advanced structural analysis

Department of Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering offers cutting-edge programs in power systems, electronics, telecommunications, and computer engineering. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to address contemporary technological challenges.

Programs Offered:

Bachelor of Technology in Electrical and Electronic Engineering

A practice-oriented program focusing on applied electrical engineering skills, electronics design, and implementation of electrical systems.

Duration: 4 Years
Delivery Mode: Full-time
Entry Requirements: C+ in KCSE
Key Subjects: Mathematics, Physics
